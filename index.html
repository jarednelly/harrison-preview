Biography

About Harrison

“For me, design is more than being creative or artsy.  It’s about innovating and developing a synergistic concept that engages viewers 
and consumers.”

Hello, and welcome to my site.  Throughout the site, you can sample my current and previous work, download my resume, and contact me directly for any questions or inquiries you may have.  I currently hold the title of Product Designer at Scorpion Internet Marketing.  I have over a decade of experience in Web, Graphic, and Product Design with an emphasis on UX/UI and responsive layouts.

I attended and graduated from The Fashion Institute of Design & Merchandising (FIDM) with an Associates Degree in Graphic Design.  At FIDM I learned the importance of meeting deadlines, presenting high quality work, and exceeding client expectations.  All these lessons I’ve taken with me in my professional journey.

For me, design is more than being creative or artsy.  It’s about innovating and developing a synergistic concept that engages viewers and consumers.  When done correctly, the result is a successful campaign, brand, or product.
